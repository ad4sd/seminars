Guide for Discussion

To better facilitate discussion, we will arrange parallel breakout rooms for different science branches. To promote live discussion amongst researchers from different disciplines, we encourage all participants to actively engage in the Open Discussion session by publicly collecting wish lists from domain scientists and feeding back to machine learning researchers. Additionally, we will curate and maintain a list of public datasets from domain scientists and public codebases from the machine learning community.
